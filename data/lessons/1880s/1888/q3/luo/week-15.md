# PUONJ 15
Oktoba 13, 1888

## TIJE SATAN

1. ANG'O ma ibiro wacho ni ji kapok Ruoth obiro? Isaya 8:19, gi kare 17.

2. Ang'o ma omiyo gidwaro joma nigi chunje gi gi? Dwoko—Mondo giwuo gi joma otho. Kare 19, giko.

3. Puonjni iluongoni nade? Dwoko—Spiritualism.

4. Be joma otho ong'eyo gimoro? Eklesiastes 9:5, 6.

5. Chunygo mage maberie ma jogi nigi, kendo mi ji luongogi mondo owuo kodgi? Fweny 16:14.

6. Ang'o ma waseyudo bedo pach maduong' mar hongigo gi wuondnigo? Fweny 13:14.

7. Ma nyiso ang'o? Dwoko—Ni Spiritualism biro tiyo maduong' ahinya kuom loso kido mar le, kendo chuno lamo mar le gi kide.

8. Ka National Reformers oyudo Yie mar Piny e Chik, to ang'o ma giwuoro? Dwoko—"We wawachi Nyasaye kaka Wuoro kod Jaloch, kod Chakruok mar Ber Duto, to gweth mare biro bedo kuomwa. Richo gi kethruok biro rumo, to loch maber mar Yesu, Ruodhwa makare, biro lochi." "Kata machalre kodgi [jo Yahudi] wadagi kendo walal, kata, bedowa pinyruoth mar Ruodhwa kod Kristone, wabiro pong'o piny kendo wabet nyaka chieng'." "To ka wachopo ewi got ... matinadi biro wuotho e ler maber ahinya mar ndalo mag higni alufu, kendo ywak biro ting'ore malo, 'Pinjruodhi mag pinyni osedoko pinjruodhi mag Ruodhwa kod Kristone.'"—New York National Reform Convention, 1873, pp. 49, 75, 47.

9. Ka giseketo mano ma giluongo ni pinyruodhe, to ang'o ma giwuoro? Dwoko—"Ka wasechopo wach marwa, kaka Kristo biro kendo tieko tichne."—Secretary J. M. Foster, e Reformed Presbyterian and Covenanter, Disemba, 1887, p. 403.

10. Gin ng'a ma biro timo ranyisi madongo kod honni mondo giwuond? Mathayo 24:24.

11. Ng'ano mogik ma biro nyisore, kendo tiyo gi, teko duto? 2 Jo-Thesalonika 2:8.

12. Kaka hongigo duto biro tim gi kristo mag miriambo, kendo kaka Satan biro timo mano maduong' moloyo, to e kit ng'a ma Satan biro nyisore e ma? Dwoko—E kit kristo mar miriambo.

13. Ka pinyruoth mar National Reform oseloki, kendo Satan, kuom tekega duto mar timo honni, dolokore maler manyiso kido mar ler, ka nochiwore kaka Kristo, to ywak malach nobed nade? Dwoko—"Kristo osebiro;" "Pinjruodhi mag pinyni osedoko pinjruodhi mag Ruodhwa kod Kristone."

14. Ng'a mabiro bedo ruodh kanyo mar National Reform Government?

15. Be Spiritualism wuoro loch manyien moro? Dwoko—Spiritualism chiko jawarna manyien, kendo nyiso biro mare "e tiengni." The World's Advance Thought riwo lwedo nyien mar chuny manyien, to e ndikone mar Aperili 5, 1886, wacho:—

"Chieng' makare machielo dwarre e piny, kendo jaote ok nyal bedo mabor ng'at mano ma tije mar ngima biro bedo timo kit adiera manyiengo ma nochiw. Ok nobi bedo Mesia mar oganda kende, kata mesia mar nus-piny, kaka notimo Nazareno maduong'; to ngwech tiyo gi mar chako yamo kod winjruok mar mil, kod winjruok mag achan mag ohala, osemiyo piny duto mar Mesia nyalore, kendo kamano e kaka achielno nobed. Kata ok ging'eyo wachni, kaka ringruok, oganda maduong' kendo mamedo mar jochunj gin joote mage." "Winjruok mar joma ok tho dwokmag yie biro miyo piny gi singo mar mesia manyien."

16. Ang'o ma jakwed wacho? Dwoko—"Koro aparo ni anyalo wacho gi ratiro ni ka wach mar National Reform loyo, kendo Nyasaye biro keto seyi gi keto luetene, eka onge kiawa moro amora kaluwore gi tekogi, joma ok oyie biro winjogi gi mor, to ka Yesu obiro kendo obedo e kom duong' makare, kama wanyalo nenoe kendo wuoyo kode, onge jong'ad yie, kendo ji duto biro winjo."—P. F. Shumaker, Flat Creek, La., e barua ne jaduok wach mar American Sentinel, Sept. 17, 1887.

17. Ang'o ma W. C. T. U. mar oganda wacho? Dwoko—"Woman's Christian Temperance Union, mag gweng', pinje, oganda kod piny mangima, nigikwano achiel maler molos chuny, paro achiel ma pong'o chuny, paro achiel mar ngima, kendo en ni Kristo nyaka bed ruoth mar pinyni. Ee, adier, ruodh pinyni e chenrone mag dwaro to ketho; ruodh buchene, lwenjene, kod ohandane; ruodh skunde kod ute mag lemo; ruodh timge kod chikege."—Union Signal, Disemba 1, 1887, p. 2.

18. Ka ikawo magi gi ogendini mamoko machielo ma kawuono timo ne wuod mar National Reform, to maromo nade yie kuom ruodh mar National Reform government?

19. Ang'o ma waseyudo michiwo mondo ores ji kuom wuondoni maduong'?

20. To ng'a kende ma notam yie ruodh mar National Reform? Dwoko—Jogo ma ohero adiera mar Ote mar Malaika mar Adek.
